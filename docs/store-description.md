# Aburrido AI — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description (132 characters max)

AI-powered autopilot for job applications. Automatically fills and submits applications on LinkedIn and Indeed using your profile.

## Full Description

Aburrido AI is an intelligent job application assistant that automates the tedious parts of your job search. Connect your AI provider, scan your LinkedIn profile, and let the extension handle the rest.

The extension reads job listings on LinkedIn and Indeed, evaluates them against your profile, and automatically fills out application forms with accurate, personalized answers powered by AI. You stay in control with configurable daily limits, salary filters, location preferences, and the ability to review pending answers before they are submitted.

Your privacy is a priority. API keys never leave your browser. All data is stored locally by default, with optional cloud sync when you sign in with Google.

### How It Works

1. Sign in with Google (optional, for cloud sync)
2. Add your AI provider API key (Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, or OpenAI)
3. Scan your LinkedIn profile or upload your resume
4. Set your preferences (daily limit, minimum salary, city, visa sponsorship)
5. Navigate to LinkedIn or Indeed job listings and click Start Autopilot

The extension handles form filling, question answering, and submission — you monitor progress from the popup or the full dashboard.

### Key Features

- Autopilot mode: automatically applies to jobs on LinkedIn and Indeed
- AI-powered answers: uses Claude, Gemini, or GPT to answer application questions based on your real profile and work history
- LinkedIn profile scanning: imports your experience, education, and skills directly from your profile page
- Resume upload: supports PDF and text resume parsing for richer AI context
- Smart job scoring: evaluates job relevance before applying so you don't waste applications
- Answer memory: saves previously answered questions for consistent, faster responses
- Pending answer review: review and edit AI-generated answers before submission
- Full dashboard: track all applications, view answers, manage your profile, and monitor AI usage
- Daily limits and filters: set application caps, minimum salary, city, and visa sponsorship requirements
- Privacy-first: API keys stored locally only; optional Google sign-in for cloud sync via Supabase
- Works across sessions: cloud sync keeps your data safe across browser clears and devices
